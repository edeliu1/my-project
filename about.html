About Us

The Smart City Web Portal of Fushe Kosova is a digital platform designed to connect citizens with municipal services in a fast, transparent, and efficient way.

Our goal is to improve everyday city life by providing easy access to healthcare, education, public transportation, municipal administration, and online document services.

By using modern web technologies, the platform supports transparency, innovation, and active citizen participation in building a smarter city.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify administrative processes, reduce waiting times, and create a user-friendly digital experience for all citizens of Fushe Kosova.

Our Vision

We envision Fushe Kosova as a fully connected smart city where technology improves quality of life, increases efficiency, and strengthens trust between citizens and local institutions.
